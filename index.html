Booking Terms & Policies

Booking Terms

  • Advance Payment: To confirm your booking, a 50% advance payment of the selected package is required. This ensures that your date is reserved exclusively for you.
  • Balance Payment: The remaining amount should be paid on the day of your event, before the service begins.
  • Cancellations: Please note all bookings are non-refundable. we recommend confirming your dates carefully to avoid inconvenience.
  • Rescheduling: In case you need to reschedule, please Inform us at least 7 days in advance . Rescheduling is subject to the availability of the makeup artist on the new date .
  • Additional Services:Any last-minute requests, changes, or extra services will incur additional charges.
  • Client Preparation: Please ensure a clean and comfortable space for the makeup session. A well-lit area with a mirror is highly recommended for best results.
  • Professional Care: Our team use high-quality, professional products for all services.we strive to make every look flawless, keeping your comfort and safety in mind.

Travel & Outstation Booking

  • Travel Charges: For bookings outside the city or requiring travel, additional charges may apply. These will be communicated and agreed upon at the time of booking.
  • Accommodation: For outstation events requiring overnight stay is needed for makeup artist, the client must provide or arrange suitable accommodation.
  • Travel Time: Travel time is calculated separately and may be included in the service hours.
  • Advance & Confirmation: A 50% advance is required to confirm the booking, including travel and accommodation costs (if applicable).
  • Cancellation / Rescheduling: Outstation bookings follow the same cancellation and rescheduling policy as local bookings.
